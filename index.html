Who We Are?

Micko is a team of five passionate electrical engineering students from Urmia University, formed with the shared goal of pushing the boundaries of technology through innovation and collaboration. With over six months of active work, Micko has already established a strong foundation in the fields of electronics and microcontroller systems, demonstrating high technical expertise and a deep commitment to quality.

Although we haven't participated in official competitions yet, our team is constantly developing and refining advanced projects, preparing for both academic and industrial challenges ahead.
